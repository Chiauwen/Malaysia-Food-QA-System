Feel Sada (Old Spelling: Feeling) or Pinaran is a traditional food for the Kadazan-Dusun people and is very popular in Sabah.This food is a type of food containing fish that has been frozen and mixed with other ingredients such as bamboo fruits, turmoil, turmeric and salt.It is also a durable food and can be stored for several days.



It is a good idea to eat with hot rice or with Ambuyat, a traditional food that is also known in Sabah.In Sabah, these foods are available at restaurants or food stalls that provide traditional Sabah Bumiputera food.There are also several leading hotels (such as Le-Meridien Hotel, Kota Kinabalu) in Sabah which began serving Pinasa for introduction to tourists coming to Sabah.
